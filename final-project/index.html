Requirements

To ensure the safety and well-being of our feline residents and the enjoyment of our visitors, we kindly ask that all visitors to our cat cafe comply with the following requirements:


  1. Sanitize your hands upon entering the cafe: This helps keep the environment clean and free of germs, which is especially important for the health of our cats.
  2. Remove your shoes: This helps keep the floors and furniture clean, as well as prevents outdoor dirt and germs from being brought into the cafe.
  3. Refrain from disturbing the cats while they're sleeping or eating: Our cats need their rest and privacy, and may become agitated or aggressive if disturbed during these times.
  4. Avoid any behaviors that could make the cats feel uncomfortable or threatened: This includes sudden movements, loud noises, or invading their personal space.
  5. Do not feed the cats: We provide food and treats for our feline residents, and giving them additional food can disrupt their diets and cause health issues.
  6. Do not pick up or carry the cats: Our cats are free to roam and interact with visitors on their own terms, and may become scared or agitated if picked up or carried.
  7. Be gentle and respectful with the cats: This includes petting them gently and following their cues for interaction, as well as refraining from any rough play or handling.

By following these requirements, we can ensure that our cats are happy and healthy, and that all visitors have a fun and enjoyable experience at our cat cafe. Thank you for your cooperation!
