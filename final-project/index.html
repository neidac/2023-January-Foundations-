About Us

Welcome to Whisker Cafe - the ultimate destination for cat lovers and coffee enthusiasts! Our cafe offers a cozy atmosphere and a variety of delicious coffee and tea beverages, as well as fresh pastries and snacks. Our resident cats are available for adoption, and we partner with local animal shelters to provide them with the best care. We believe in giving back to the community and donate a portion of our profits to local animal shelters and rescue organizations. Visit us today and enjoy a cup of coffee with our furry friends!

Adoption

Our adoption process is designed to ensure that our cats are placed in loving and caring homes. If you want to adopt one of our cats, fill out an adoption application. Our team will review your application and contact you to schedule a meet and greet with the cat of your choice. During your visit, you can spend time with the cat, ask questions, and get to know its unique personality. If you feel a connection with the cat, we will work with you to complete the adoption process. We believe that every cat deserves a loving home, and we are committed to helping our cats find their forever families. If you're interested in adopting one of our cats, simply click the "Adopt Now" button on our website to view a list of available cats

Requirements

To ensure the safety and well-being of our feline residents and the enjoyment of our visitors, we kindly ask that all visitors to our cat cafe comply with the following requirements:


  1. Sanitize your hands upon entering the cafe: This helps keep the environment clean and free of germs, which is especially important for the health of our cats.
  2. Remove your shoes: This helps keep the floors and furniture clean, as well as prevents outdoor dirt and germs from being brought into the cafe.
  3. Refrain from disturbing the cats while they're sleeping or eating: Our cats need their rest and privacy, and may become agitated or aggressive if disturbed during these times.
  4. Avoid any behaviors that could make the cats feel uncomfortable or threatened: This includes sudden movements, loud noises, or invading their personal space.
  5. Do not feed the cats: We provide food and treats for our feline residents, and giving them additional food can disrupt their diets and cause health issues.
  6. Do not pick up or carry the cats: Our cats are free to roam and interact with visitors on their own terms, and may become scared or agitated if picked up or carried.
  7. Be gentle and respectful with the cats: This includes petting them gently and following their cues for interaction, as well as refraining from any rough play or handling.

By following these requirements, we can ensure that our cats are happy and healthy, and that all visitors have a fun and enjoyable experience at our cat cafe. Thank you for your cooperation!

Meet Our Cats

Our adoptable cats are fully vetted and up-to-date on their vaccinations, and they receive the same level of care and attention as our resident cats. We are always excited to see one of our adoptable cats find their forever family, and we celebrate each successful adoption with joy and gratitude.
