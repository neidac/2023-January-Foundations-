About Us

Welcome to Whisker Cafe - the ultimate destination for cat lovers and coffee enthusiasts! Our cafe offers a cozy atmosphere and a variety of delicious coffee and tea beverages, as well as fresh pastries and snacks. Our resident cats are available for adoption, and we partner with local animal shelters to provide them with the best care. We believe in giving back to the community and donate a portion of our profits to local animal shelters and rescue organizations. Visit us today and enjoy a cup of coffee with our furry friends!

Adoption

Our adoption process is designed to ensure that our cats are placed in loving and caring homes. If you want to adopt one of our cats, fill out an adoption application. Our team will review your application and contact you to schedule a meet and greet with the cat of your choice. During your visit, you can spend time with the cat, ask questions, and get to know its unique personality. If you feel a connection with the cat, we will work with you to complete the adoption process. We believe that every cat deserves a loving home, and we are committed to helping our cats find their forever families. If you're interested in adopting one of our cats, simply click the "Adopt Now" button on our website to view a list of available cats
